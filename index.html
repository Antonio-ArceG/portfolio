PUBLICATIONS

Social impact assessments: Developing a consolidated conceptual framework (peer-reviewed)

Arce-Gomez, Antonio., Donovan, J. D., & Bedggood, R. E. (2015). Social impact assessments: Developing a consolidated conceptual framework. Environmental Impact Assessment Review, 50, 85-94

ABOUT THIS ARTICLE

This article attempts to synthesise the most recent theoretical and practical developments in the field of Social Impact Assessment (SIA) into one conceptual framework. This was done with the intention of leading to further efforts towards a meta-evaluation of SIA literature and a platform from which newer developments may be further investigated. This article was published in the leading academic journal for Impact Assessment studies and written based on my Honours thesis and a previous conference paper.

Developing a ‘best practice’ SIA process: Exploring the integration of technical and participatory approaches (conference paper)

Arce-Gomez, Antonio, Donovan, J. D., & Bedggood, R. E. (2013). Developing a ‘best practice’ SIA process: Exploring the integration of technical and participatory approaches. In IAIA13 Conference Proceedings, Impact Assessment the NextGeneration, 33rd Annual Meeting of the International Association for Impact Assessment, Calgary, Canada

ABOUT THIS ARTICLE

This conference paper explores the potential for integration between technical and participatory approaches to SIA with a particular focus on their use by businesses. Its aim is to lay the foundations for a ‘best practice’ SIA process. This paper was written based on my Honours thesis and was presented at an international conference for Impact Assessment (IA) practitioners and academics (Canada, 2013).
